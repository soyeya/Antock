• Best Practice Benchmarking

  • Victory Road selected 30 past success cases
    from the IBK database and analyzed the historical
    growth trajectories of the chosen SMEs to identify
    common success patterns.

  • Growth Driver Extraction

  • Through the benchmarking process,
    16 common key growth drivers and 135 potential
    data variables were extracted from the
    alternative data library.

  • Growth DNA Validation

  • A total of 44 variables were validated as viable
    growth factors in the robotics sector, and the
    individual weight of each variable was calculated
    for use in the prediction model.

  • Startup Prospect Curation

  • The growth prediction model was applied
    to 74,394 companies in the IBK database,
    and the top 30 companies from the robotics
    sector were selected based on their
    respective scores.
